What We Do

Still Has Value turns your no-longer-needed items into someone else's perfect find—without the hassle, guilt, or waste. For you (the seller): Schedule a free, quick pickup at your convenience. We get it out of your way fast and you can feel great knowing your unwanted item isn't heading to a landfill. Instead, we revive it and help put it into the hands of someone in different circumstances. No more worrying about it sitting unappreciated or ending up as trash. For buyers: Discover real deals on quality, estranged items that have plenty of value to the right person. We only list pieces worth keeping—cleaned, repaired if needed, and honestly described—so you shop with confidence. No junk, no surprises—just great value that saves you money and keeps good things in circulation. It's simple sustainability: one happy goodbye, one excited hello, zero waste.
